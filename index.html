Battle Mode is Thinkle's multiplayer quiz system. You can compete in real-time quiz battles with friends or random rivals. Up to 6 players can compete in the same room.

How to Play: Create a room → Choose a category (General Knowledge, Driving Test, Science, History, Movies, Music and more) → Share the 6-character room code → Once everyone clicks "Ready," the host starts → 10 questions, 20 seconds each → Highest score wins!

Scoring: Each correct answer gives 100 points. Speed bonus applies — the faster you answer, the more bonus points (max +50). Wrong answers don't deduct points.

Friend Invite: From the Friends panel, you can directly invite online friends using the ⚔️ button. Your friend receives a notification on their screen and joins with one tap.
