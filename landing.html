How It Works

From File to Lesson in Seconds

No setup. No registration required. Just upload your file and Thinkle handles the rest.

STEP 01
📁

Upload Your File

PDF, Word, Excel, CSV, TXT or a photo. Thinkle reads and analyzes every format including handwritten notes.

STEP 02
🧠

AI Analyzes It

Thinkle breaks the content into structured slides, adds 5-7 detailed bullet points per topic and rich explanations.

A 10-question auto-generated quiz to measure what you've learned. Missed topics get detailed re-teaching slides.
